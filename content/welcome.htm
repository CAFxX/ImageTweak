ImageTweak 0.22

Thank you for installing ImageTweak.

The box on the right is a demonstration of the ImageTweak viewer. Put the mouse pointer over it and zoom the image by using ctrl+mousewheel, ctrl+-/+. Rotate the image by using ctrl+alt+mousewheel or ctrl+</>. Press 1 to have the image fitted to the box, 2 to have it fill the box.

To learn more about what you can do with ImageTweak, read the user guide. To customize your settings, edit your ImageTweak preferences.
